# Pregame The Game
- You leave your apartment, booze in tow, and you must maintain just the riiiiight amount of tipsy throughout the walk without getting caught. Taking sneaky, cautious sips just as each pedestrian passes by and steering clear of these damn traffic cones. What genius put them in your way anyway? Will you get to the bar perfectly pre-gamed?
- Play it here on [Itch.io](https://tokkebee.itch.io/pregame-the-game)
